Cooked to Catastrophe!

September - December 2020

Learn to cook and save the world! Centuries after the apocalypse, a group of scavengers stumbles across an artifact of unimaginable power: the Infinite Pantry. With unending ingredients at your disposal, you’ve opened The Last Stop, a restaurant to feed your fellow survivors. Learn to cook real recipes and access them anywhere from your phone!

Over the course of one semester five of my peers and I created the game Cooked to Catastrophe! While this game is far from complete, I learned a lot from this project. Within the course this project was assigned we learned how to use the Scrum method and how benifical it can be for development. These concepts are ones that I plan on including in any future project that I work on.

Grave Escape (Wrath of the Boney Boys)

October 2019

Grave Escape is a comedy horror game that four classmates and I created over a five week period in one of our academic courses. The main game mechanics are centered around your character finding and collecting pumpkins while you are being chased by skeletons that are ragdolling and chasing after the player. I created the main enemy AI, the level design, and worked on the collectables.
