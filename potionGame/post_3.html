Intro

This sprint's post is going to be a bit of a smaller one as I wasn't able to work on the game a ton last week. I'm still working out the art style and I think I'm close. I created some temp assets for the time being just to have something that aren't isometric diamonds and cubes.

Moodboards

I created two moodboards to try and get a good understanding of the style that I want. I think I am going to go the pixel art route as it's what I know best and I think I can come up with something pretty solid using it. The moodboard on the left showcases some of the games that I want to emulate the style/feeling with my game. The first thing is that I want the game to feel cozy so I looked at games like Little Witch in the Woods and Glimmerwick as examples. The moodboard on the right demonstrates more of the exact isometric feel I'm aiming for.
