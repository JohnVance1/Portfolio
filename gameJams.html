Game Jams

Meld

March 2023

I created a 2D platformer where the player controls a cat who can hide in similarly colored objects. The game was created over 20 hours of a 48 hour game jam, the 127 Mini Jam, with the theme being cats2 and a color limitation of six colors. The goal is to make it to the other side of the room without being caught.

Mush Ants

April 2023

Mush Ants is a simple game about playing as a mushroom trying to destroy your local ant colony. Move around the map spray a poison cloud, destroying any living ant or anthill in your wake. Once all of the ants are defeated the timer will stop and you have successfully decimated their society.

Submitted to the Mini Jam 129 - Poison game jam.

You can play the game on itch.io below

Crunchtime Jam - Cyberpunk

November 2022

I created a cyberpunk track for the OST Composing: Crunchtime2 Jam. The track is not current being used on a game however, while I created the track i saw it being used in a cyberpunk game around the player waking up and finding most of their missing organs replaced with robotic parts. The overall goal of the game is to retrieve them from various bosses(think mega man style) and this section would be when the player is looking for their heart.
