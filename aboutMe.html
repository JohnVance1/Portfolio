About Me

I recently graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a Master of Science degree in Game Design and Development with a minor in Music and Technology.

My goal to combine my two passions of coding and composing while also having a well rounded skill set in game design. I am very passionate about Game Design and I actively seek out new techniques and information I can use when creating games.

Throughout my time at RIT I have played in both the concert and jazz bands at RIT, participated in multiple volleyball intermural leauges simultaniously, and I was the President of the RIT Juggling Club for three years.
