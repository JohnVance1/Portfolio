Game Jams

Meld

March 2023

I created a 2D platformer where the player controls a cat who can hide in similarly colored objects. The game was created over 20 hours of a 48 hour game jam, the 127 Mini Jam, with the theme being cats2 and a color limitation of six colors. The goal is to make it to the other side of the room without being caught.
